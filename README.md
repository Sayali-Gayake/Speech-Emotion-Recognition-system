#  Speech Emotion Recognition for Early Detection of Depression and Mental Health Issues

##  Abstract
Depression is one of the most common mental health disorders and often remains undiagnosed due to the lack of early detection methods. This project aims to develop a Speech Emotion Recognition (SER) system that analyzes speech signals to detect emotional states that may indicate early signs of depression. The system extracts Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCC) from audio samples and uses machine learning models to classify emotions, helping enable AI-based mental health monitoring.
